College Seat Finder:

Getting admission into a college after 12th is not as simple as it looks. In Every college there are different variety of seats based on different reservation category with different fees structure and all for the same course. To make it more complex you can write many entrance exams to get admission into same college for same course.

Under every entrance exam there are tens of reservation categories. Further, these reservation categories are very complex in nature as they are based on religion, caste, language, region, parent’s profession etc… Under the regulations by the Government each college should reserve certain percentage of seats for different categories.

First of all, finding which reservation category you belong to is a great challenge faced by students, above that student are left with the further challenge that in which college they have reserved seats for their preferred course and how many number of reserved seats.

So, we bring you this technology where you can find out college wise and course wise seat allocation:

  • Total number of reserved seats available for selected course in a college.
  • Category wise number of reserved seats for a selected course in each college.
  • Total number of seats available in particular college for particular course (approved intake).
  • Number of seats available for direct admission under management quota in a college.
