12th Science Student? Writing KCET, NEET, ComedK entrance Exam? Looking for admission to Medical, Engineering, Nursing, Paramedical courses? Seeking admission to Top Best Colleges in Karnataka?

Every year 12th Students, especially Science students face lot dilemma about their future? First, they have to make their career choice-Medical, Engineering, Allied Health Science etc…. Then they have to write lot of entrance exams like JEE, NEET, KCET, ComedK etc and try to get a few seats available in reputed colleges.

Admission procedure under entrance test in Indian Education System is very complex. There are tens and hundreds of reservation quotas based on caste, religion, language, profession etc. Each college has limited intake of seats and majority percentage of seats are reserved as per government regulations. These reserved seats are further divided among different reservation categories.

So, each college end up with having their seat distributed among lots of reservation category. Moreover, admission to these seats is done based on the rankings obtained in entrance exam by the student. Each reservation category seat has different fees structure to be paid by the students.

Now students are confused – Which college has how many numbers of seats reserved for which category? What is the cut off rank for different reservation category? What is the fees structure for various reservation category?

We at Career University help students to clarify these confusions by bringing them technology solutions which provide them information they require at their fingertips.

  3. How many seats are available for direct admission under management quota seats in a college?
  4. How many reserved seats are available for selected course in their targeted college?
  5. What is the cutoff rank for their selected course in their targeted college for various reservation category?
  6. What is the fees structure for the course selected in various colleges for different reservation category?
  7. What is the management quota fees structure for a particular course in a selected college?
